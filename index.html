Boating through thekkady dam

The boat ride lasted about 2 hours and we saw plenty of birds, some mammals and the occasional reptile. While there was nothing spectacular, it was a pleasant way to spend a couple of hours..

The Periyar lake harbors a unique aquatic ecosystem and the land surrounding the lake is commonly frequented by wildlife. Boat rides usually span a duration of one to two hours and typical timings are 7.30 AM, 9.30 AM, 11.15 AM, 1.45 PM, 3.30 PM. These numbers tentative and subject to the discretion of the organizers.

About

WELCOME TO PERIYAR TIGER RESERVE THEKKADY PERIYAR TIGER RESERVE (PTR) is one of the most visited protected areas in India, bagged the title of the Best managed Tiger Reserve among 54 Tiger Reserves in the country in terms of Management Effectiveness Assessment in 2022 by National t The diversity of wildlife includes Indian Elephant (Elephas maximus) , Bengal Tiger (Panthera tigris), Sambar (Cervus unicolor).

Nature

A living canvas of nature's artistry, where the emerald hues of the forest meet the tranquil blues of its pristine lake. Here, amidst the rustling leaves and melodious bird songs, you'll encounter the elusive tigers, herds of elephants, and a diverse array of wildlife. Delve into the heart of this ecological sanctuary, where every moment is an opportunity to witness the timeless beauty of Periyar in all its glory.

World Heritage Site

Periyar Tiger Reserve is also part of the World Heritage site “Western Ghats” under UNESCO. PTR achieved 23 awards and recognitions for excellence in diverse fields that reflect our commitment to conservation and social responsibility. Vasnthasena the unique women's voluntary Group in PTR, participate in the protection works by offering their services in sandalwood patrolling and Plastic Cleaning.

How to Reach Thekkady : Your Travel Guide to Accessing Natural Splendor

Madurai Airport

Nearest Airport: Madurai, about 140 Km

Cochin International Airport

International Airport, about 190 Km from Thekkady

Kottayam Railway Station

Nearest Railway Station is the Kottayam Railway Station, which is about 110 Km from Thekkady

State Bus

State Buses operate on a regular basis from the nearby cities like Kottayam, Kochi and Trivandrum.
