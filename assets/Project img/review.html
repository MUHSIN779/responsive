welcome to
Periyar Tiger Reserve Thekkady
PERIYAR TIGER RESERVE (PTR) is one of the most visited protected areas in India, bagged the title of the Best managed Tiger Reserve among 54 Tiger Reserves in the country in terms of Management Effectiveness Assessment in 2022 by National t The diversity of wildlife includes Indian Elephant (Elephas maximus) , Bengal Tiger (Panthera tigris), Sambar (Cervus unicolor), Gaur (Bos gaurus), Leopard (Prionailurus bengalensis), Indian Wild dog (Cuon alpinus), Barking deer (Muntiacus muntjak), Mouse deer (Tragulus meminna), Monitor lizard (Varanus bengalensis) and a variety of birds and reptile species. PTR is rich in endemism with three unique species of flora and seven species of Fishes. PTR is situated in the 37' 10.2” N and longitudes 76° 56' 12.12” and 77° 25' 5.52” E. Administratively, PTR falls in the Idukki, Cardamom Hills and Pandalam Hills of the Southern Western Ghats between latitudes 9° 17' 56.04” and 90 Kottayam, and Pathanamthitta districts of Kerala. Periyar Tiger Reserve Unveils Nature's Masterpiece, Where The Emerald Hues Of The Forest Meet
The Tranquil Blues Of Its Pristine Lake
A living canvas of nature's artistry, where the emerald hues of the forest meet the tranquil blues of its pristine lake. Here, amidst the rustling leaves and melodious bird songs, you'll encounter the elusive tigers, herds of elephants, and a diverse array of wildlife. Delve into the heart of this ecological sanctuary, where every moment is an opportunity to witness the timeless beauty of Periyar in all its glory.

Periyar Tiger Reserve - World Heritage Site
The reserve is well known for its unique and diverse flora
Periyar Tiger Reserve is also part of the World Heritage site “Western Ghats” under UNESCO. PTR achieved 23 awards and recognitions for excellence in diverse fields that reflect our commitment to conservation and social responsibility. How to Reach Thekkady : Your Travel Guide to Accessing Natural Splendor
Nearest Airport: Madurai, about 140 Km and Cochin International Airport, about 190 Km from Thekkady
Nearest Railway Station is the Kottayam Railway Station, which is about 110 Km from Thekkady
State Buses operate on a regular basis from the nearby cities like Kottayam, Kochi and Trivandrum.
